Thief Caught in Downtown London Robbery
Police apprehended a suspect following a robbery in central London. Authorities are investigating the incident and reviewing CCTV footage.

According to witnesses, the thief entered a jewelry store at around 10:30 AM and attempted to steal valuable items. The store owner quickly alerted nearby security, and the police arrived within minutes. The suspect was caught trying to flee the scene.

Authorities have urged citizens to stay vigilant and report any suspicious activity in the area. CCTV footage is being reviewed to ensure no accomplices were involved.
