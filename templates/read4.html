Empowering Women in Tech
The technology sector has long been dominated by men, but the tide is shifting. Across the globe, women are breaking barriers, leading innovative projects, and inspiring the next generation of tech professionals.

Initiatives promoting gender diversity have helped women access mentorship, scholarships, and leadership roles in startups and large corporations alike. Programs like *Women Who Code* and *Girls in Tech* are creating platforms for women to collaborate, learn, and thrive in competitive industries.
