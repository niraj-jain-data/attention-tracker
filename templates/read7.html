Accident Disrupts London Metro Services
A collision on the London Underground this morning has caused significant delays across multiple lines. Commuters experienced long wait times, and many were advised to seek alternative transport options.

Authorities are currently investigating the incident. Preliminary reports suggest a minor collision between two trains at a central station during peak hours. Emergency response teams arrived promptly to ensure passenger safety.

Transport for London (TfL) has issued updates recommending alternate routes and encouraging passengers to allow extra travel time. Services are expected to resume normal operations once safety checks are complete.

Commuters are urged to stay informed via official TfL announcements and exercise caution while navigating the affected stations.
